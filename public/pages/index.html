ROOTED IN COMMUNITY.
DRIVEN BY CARE.

You deserve to feel safe, supported, and empowered in your community. Whether you're seeking guidance on your rights, looking for local services, or simply need to know where to turn, we're here for you. Our commitment is to provide accessible and timely information in a space where you are valued and your needs matter. Charlotte is stronger when all our communities thrive. We stand with you and are dedicated to helping you access the support available in our community.

FINDING YOUR PATH FORWARD

Legal questions don't come with simple answers and they shouldn't come with barriers either. Whether you're navigating your first steps toward residency, seeking protection for yourself or your family, or ready to build your future, you deserve access to information in a language you understand. This library brings together helpful resources all in one place. We've collected guides, explanations, and tools from trusted sources to help you learn about your options.

SOMEONE ON YOUR SIDE

Reading about your rights is helpful. Having someone in your corner who knows the community and understands the unique challenges in your area? That changes everything. We've listed attorneys and organizations who focus on immigration law and serve communities like ours. Use the map to find attorneys near you.
